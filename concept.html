Everything can be seen as a dot.
Dots can make lines and lines can make space.

However, sometimes we just see dots seperately,
and even see ourselves as isolated ones.

That’s why xyxy wants to use her design power
to start or restart communicating with the dots, the lines and the space.

She’d like all the people who’ve encountered her designs to feel connected and become a member of the journey in the space.

Everyone deserves some shiny time, like a star in the space.

That is her passion... and her job.
