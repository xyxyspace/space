Fly The Kite High

2020

This project is designed for the International Kite Festival, for encouraging more people to know about kites and enjoy this activity. The idea is to use the AR technology helping people flying kites.

It is quite easy to fly the kite - just run while taking selfies. If you run fast enough, a random kite will fly up. If you keep running, the kite will stay in the sky. The kite will drop if you are too slow.

Fly more and fly high.*

*Never fly your kite in the traffic. Safety always comes the first.
