Service Design

Client: AusPost

This proposal was on the short list
for presenting in AusPost in 2018.

In this three-week project, xyxy and her two other team members used service design tools and methods to do a series of brainstorming, interviews, and data analysis. At the end of the project, they provided the concept ideations and the relatively future products.

Her shining moments:

xyxy was the interviewer, the observer and the presenter of this project. She interviewed 11 people from different countries and provided her understanding of the personal identity. She is also passionate about showing the abstract concept with simple illustrations and pictures. Jokes were also included.
